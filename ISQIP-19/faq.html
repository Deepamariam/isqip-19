Who can attend ISQIP?

Everyone who wants to acquire a new skill in programming, electronics, electrical or instrumentation can attend ISQIP.

Which all workshops can I attend in CS ISQIP?

The workshops in the first 2 days is mandatory. You can choose between 2 different workshops on day 3 . Core workshops on day 4, 5 & 6 has 4 options and you can opt the one you are more interested in.

Will I be admitted to the workshops I choose?

Workshops are assigned in First-come-first-served method. So be fast and register for ISQIP quick. ;)

Will certificates be provided?

Certificates will be provided to all the delegates. Separate cerificate will be given for hackathon.

Is attendence for hackathon mandatory?

Yes. You will be provided the certificates only if you attend the hackathon

No

Which company will i intern with?

We have contacted various companies for internships and they will be providing internships for the students who performs good in ISQIP 2019.

Can i attend the program if i have no basics in coding?

The foremost prerequisite to attend ISQIP is a determined mind to learn new things.We start from the basics, so it will be fine if you aren't an experienced coder.

Are there any conditions to appear for the internship drive?

Interns are to be selected solely on the performance in hackathon. So it is necessary to attend hackathon if you want to get an internship.

Why should I attend workshops like ISQIP?

Whatever taught at ISQIP and other workshops might not be included in your curriculum, but these will definetly help you when you attend a placement drive and furthur to your proffesional future. Because you attain more skills and knowledge which makes you exceptional out of the thousands of engineers passing out every year.

What if I don't have an idea for hackathon?

That’s normal. Most people don’t have an idea before they get to the event. But once you start talking to other people, you might come up with something. You can also work with somebody else on their idea if you like it.
